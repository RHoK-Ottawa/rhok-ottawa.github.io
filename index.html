Amnesty International Canada

Amnesty International is a global movement of over 4.6 million people committed to defending those who are denied justice or freedom. http://amnesty.ca

Project details

Petitions are a great tactic, but there's some crucial functionality - and fun - lost in the switch from paper to digital. The Petition App will help activists search existing e-petitions, create new petitions, collect signatures, and submit petitions to Amnesty International (which delivers the petition).

Calling all designers, front-end and back-end developers!

Come join us in working on interesting projects solving real-world problems. The cost is free to participate. We provide the space to hack, and our generous sponsors are covering 5 meals throughout the weekend.
