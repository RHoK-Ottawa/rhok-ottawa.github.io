Random Hacks of Kindness Ottawa is currently looking for organizations to participate in our bi-annual event.

Don't worry if you don't have any ideas right away, we have lots of experience ironing out productive, actionable roadmaps for a weekend long event with future growth and development in mind.

Random Hacks of Kindness Ottawa is part of a global movement that organizes hackathons where problem solvers, creative thinkers, and technologists get together for a weekend to start solving problems faced by charities and civic organizations. Over the course of a weekend, teams of 4 to 10 people bring their creative, technical, and problem solving skills to discuss ideas, design solutions and develop working prototypes for a variety of real world challenges.

Friday May 23 - Sunday May 25

Will be the fourth event for the Random Hacks of Kindness Ottawa Chapter. Over 100 people have participated and collaborated to solve over 20 organizations unique challenges.

In order to work on solving a problem, we need a stakeholder there who understands the context and environment a successful project would work in. As such, there should be a representative from your organization present for the length of the event. It doesn't have to be the same person, but teams should have someone who can answer their questions.
