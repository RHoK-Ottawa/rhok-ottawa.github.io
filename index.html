Girltank

Girltank is a movement inspired by young women leaders – from 6 continents and approximately 30 countries – who are already making a difference for their communities, standing up for causes they believe in, and using their creativity and determination to bring forth transformative and long-lasting initiatives for the people and communities they care about.

Our mission is to inspire, connect and fund female social entrepreneurs and change makers who have bold and transformative social innovation projects.
