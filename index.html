Hello, Ottawa!


On June 1st and 2nd, Shopify is participating in a global hackathon event hosted by Random Hacks of Kindness. We want to bring together Ottawa’s tech community and our international development community and make something awesome. Our theme for this event is gender equality and we’re looking for subject matter experts who want to attend the event and help people build solutions to complicated and pressing problems. For example, a 2011 RHoK hackathon in montreal produced a working app for android to help researchers count bacterial colonies in water samples.

If you have an idea for a project or are interested in providing sponsorship, please get in touch.
